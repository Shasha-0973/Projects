HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the backbone of a portfolio website, used to structure and organize all the content such as your name, projects, skills, experience, contact information, and more. It defines what appears on the page and how it's laid out, making it the starting point for building an online presence...

CSS

CSS is used to style and design your portfolio website. While HTML gives your portfolio structure, CSS makes it look good controlling layout, colors, fonts, spacing, and responsiveness.Adds visual appeal to your projects and content...

Javascript
